REFUND & CANCELLATION POLICY

At Imaginary Hub, we strive to provide a seamless experience for our customers. However, please note the following refund and cancellation policy:

There is a strict no refund and no cancellation policy for all our courses and services.

Exception

You are entitled to a refund only in the case where you have not been allotted the course after payment. In such a situation, we will initiate a refund process to return the amount paid by you.
